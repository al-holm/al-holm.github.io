About

I work on language models — compressing them, editing their knowledge, and trying to understand what's going on inside. Currently doing a CS master's at TUM and working on model compression for child safety at Helmit.

Before this I studied philosophy at Saint Petersburg University, did interpretability research at King's College London, and worked on knowledge editing at hessian.AI. My bachelor's thesis at Marburg was on pruning-based approaches for reversing ROME edits.

This is where I write things down. Sometimes about research, sometimes about other stuff. It's mostly a notebook.

If something here is wrong or interesting, feel free to tell me.
